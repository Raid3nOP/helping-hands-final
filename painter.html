Specialty Coatings

Painters specialize in applying specialty coatings and finishes for specific purposes, such as waterproofing, fireproofing, anti-graffiti coatings, non-slip coatings, and high-temperature coatings. These coatings provide additional protection and functionality beyond standard paint.

Surface Preparation

Painters prepare surfaces by cleaning, sanding, patching, caulking, and priming them to ensure proper adhesion and a smooth finish. They may also remove old paint, wallpaper, or other coatings as needed.

Decorative Painting

Specialize in creating decorative effects and artistic finishes on walls, ceilings, and other surfaces. They use techniques such as faux painting, stenciling, sponging, ragging, and murals to add texture, depth, and visual interest to interior spaces.
