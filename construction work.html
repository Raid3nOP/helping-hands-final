Structural Framing

Involves erecting the framework of a building or structure. This may include assembling steel or timber frames, installing floor joists, wall studs, and roof trusses, and securing them in place to create the skeleton of the building.

Roofing and Siding

Roofing and siding work involves installing roofing materials, such as shingles, tiles, or metal panels, to protect the building from the elements. Siding installation involves applying exterior cladding materials, such as vinyl, wood, or fiber cement, to the walls of the building for insulation and aesthetics.

Interior Finishing

Interior finishing involves installing interior elements and finishes to complete the building's interior spaces. This may include hanging drywall, installing flooring, trim, doors, and windows, painting, and applying decorative finishes to walls and ceilings.
