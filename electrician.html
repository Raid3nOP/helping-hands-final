Commercial Electrical Work

Commercial electricians work on electrical systems in commercial buildings, offices, retail stores, restaurants, and other non-residential settings.

Construction Electrical Work

Electricians are responsible for installing electrical systems in new construction projects, including residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. This involves reading blueprints, running wiring, installing electrical fixtures, and ensuring compliance with building codes and safety regulations.

Maintenance and Repair

Electricians perform routine maintenance and repair work to ensure the safety and functionality of electrical systems. This may involve troubleshooting electrical problems, diagnosing issues with wiring or equipment, repairing faulty components, and replacing worn-out or damaged parts.
